Why Malaria ?

Malaria is a life-threatening disease dominant in developing countries. According to WHO, in 2018, nearly half of the world's population was at risk of malaria with more than 90 countries reporting malaria cases and Africa was home to 435,000 deaths. Also, they report that malaria kills a child every 2 minutes. Nevertheless, prompt diagnosis and treatment can help alleviate such circumstances.
